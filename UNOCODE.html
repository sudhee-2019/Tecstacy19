Description

This game can be played by 2 or more people with a maxiumum of 5 .
Each player will be given a set of cards. These cards include number cards as well as wild cards(+2 or +4). The player can avoid taking the extra cards by solving a question which include outputs and simple codes.
The difficulty level of given question will be based on the wild card. +2 -> basic level question +4 -> intermediate level question If the person feels that the oppent is going to win , he or she can solve a code. And if the code is correct, then the opponent will be given extra cards(+5). Winner will be decided based on the no. of questions/codes solved and their difficulty level.
Venue - Outdoor(L-Block)
